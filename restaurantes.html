Sushi Fest Barranquilla is an interactive culinary festival where the city's top restaurants present an exclusive signature roll created only for the event. The public votes and decides the winner.

We celebrate culinary excellence through creativity, honoring cultural identity and crafting memorable experiences, with a genuine commitment to sustainability and community.
